Tulasi (Holy Basil)

Scientific Name: Ocimum tenuiflorum

Uses: Tulasi is widely used in Ayurveda for boosting immunity, relieving stress, and improving respiratory health.

Areas Found: Native to India and widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Helps in reducing fever and managing respiratory disorders.
  • Possesses antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Supports oral health and aids digestion.
  • Used as an adaptogen to combat stress.
