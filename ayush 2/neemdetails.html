Neem

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

Uses: Neem is used in Ayurveda for skin care, detoxification, and boosting immunity.

Areas Found: Native to India and widely grown in tropical and subtropical regions.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Supports healthy skin and prevents acne.
  • Helps in managing blood sugar levels.
  • Has antifungal and antibacterial properties.
  • Promotes oral health and reduces inflammation.
