Henna

Scientific Name: Lawsonia inermis

Uses: Henna is used as a natural dye for hair and skin, and for its cooling properties.

Areas Found: Native to North Africa and South Asia.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Conditions and strengthens hair.
  • Soothes burns and skin irritations.
  • Acts as a natural cooling agent.
  • Reduces headaches and inflammation.
