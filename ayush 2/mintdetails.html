Mint

Scientific Name: Mentha

Uses: Mint is used in culinary dishes, beverages, and traditional medicine for its refreshing properties.

Areas Found: Grown worldwide, especially in temperate climates.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Relieves indigestion and nausea.
  • Soothes headaches and muscle pain.
  • Improves breath freshness and oral health.
  • Reduces stress and promotes relaxation.
