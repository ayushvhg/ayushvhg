Fenugreek

Scientific Name: Trigonella foenum-graecum

Uses: Fenugreek is used in culinary dishes, herbal medicine, and as a dietary supplement.

Areas Found: Native to the Mediterranean region and widely cultivated in Asia and Europe.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Improves digestion and appetite.
  • Supports lactation in nursing mothers.
  • Helps regulate blood sugar levels.
  • Boosts testosterone and libido.
