Ginger

Scientific Name: Zingiber officinale

Uses: Ginger is widely used for treating nausea, improving digestion, and reducing inflammation.

Areas Found: Grown in tropical and subtropical regions, especially in India and China.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Relieves nausea and morning sickness.
  • Improves digestion and reduces bloating.
  • Helps reduce muscle pain and soreness.
  • Supports immune health.
