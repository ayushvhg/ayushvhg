Aloe Vera

Scientific Name: Aloe barbadensis miller

Uses: Aloe Vera is widely used in skincare products, beverages, and traditional medicine.

Areas Found: Grown in arid and tropical regions around the world.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Heals burns and soothes skin irritations.
  • Improves digestion and reduces constipation.
  • Boosts hydration and skin elasticity.
  • Rich in vitamins and antioxidants.
